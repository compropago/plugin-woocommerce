This extension allows you to accept payments in WooCoomerce via Compropago.com, 
the easiest way to accept credit cards online. The extension can also store customer 
and card information in your Compropago account, allowing your customers to utilize their stored card 
information for future purchases in your store.

Installing

1. Download the plugin zip file from: https://github.com/compropago/plugin-woocommerce
2. Login to your WordPress Admin. Click on Plugins | Add New from the left hand menu
3. Click on the “Upload” option, then click “Browse” to select the zip file from your computer. Once selected, press “OK” and press the “Install Now” button.
4. Activate the plugin.
